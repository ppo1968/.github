# SportsLab Research

Developing open-source research software and reproducible digital technologies for sports science, health, exercise physiology and artificial intelligence.

---

## Who We Are

SportsLab Research is an independent research initiative dedicated to developing scientific software and digital technologies for sports science, health sciences and human performance.

Our mission is to promote open science by creating reliable, reproducible and well-documented research software that supports researchers, clinicians, practitioners and students worldwide.

---

## Research Areas

- Sports Science
- Exercise Physiology
- Wearable Technologies
- Artificial Intelligence
- Digital Health
- Biomechanics
- Human Performance
- Research Software Engineering
